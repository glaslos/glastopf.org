Glastopf Project

edited by Lukas Rist on April 2. 2012

The Glastopf Project is a small group of people working together to fight against cybercrime.
To increase our knowledge we are trying to share our findings with as many people as possible:

Cooperations

  • Affiliated to the Honeynet Project since many years
  • Close collaboration with the 1&1 abuse team
  • Web Application Honeypot: Google Summer of Code 2009 supervised by the Honeynet Project and guided by Thorsten Holz
  • SURFnet plug-in to send data to the SURFids Distributed Intrusion Detection System
  • Collaboration with Project Honeypot
  • Digitalus Webhosting & Webdesign
  • In contact with HostExploit.com
